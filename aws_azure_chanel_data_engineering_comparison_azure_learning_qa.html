Azure / Fabric Learning Center

Learn Microsoft Fabric through the AWS services and data-engineering patterns you already know.用你熟悉的 AWS、Spark、Hive、Iceberg 和数仓经验理解 Azure/Fabric。

First mental model: Microsoft Fabric is not one database. It is an integrated analytics platform where ingestion, Spark, SQL warehouse, semantic models, Power BI and governance work over OneLake.Fabric 不是一个数据库，而是围绕 OneLake 整合数据接入、Spark、SQL 数仓、语义层、Power BI 和治理的平台。
Picture 1 — The Fabric building

Workspace

A collaboration and security container holding lakehouses, warehouses, pipelines, notebooks, semantic models and reports.类似项目空间，放置数据资产并管理成员和权限。

AWS analogy: project/account boundary

Capacity

The shared compute pool that runs Fabric workloads. Workspaces are assigned to a capacity.Fabric 的共享算力池，Workspace 运行在 Capacity 上。

Think: shared compute budget

OneLake

The organization-wide logical lake used by Fabric workloads. Data can be accessed by multiple engines without creating many copies.统一逻辑数据湖，多种计算引擎共享同一份数据。

Closest concept: Amazon S3

Lakehouse

Delta tables and files in OneLake, used through Spark and a SQL analytics endpoint. Best for engineering and mixed structured/unstructured data.适合 Spark 数据工程、Delta 表和结构化/非结构化混合数据。

S3 + Iceberg + EMR/Athena

Warehouse

A relational, T-SQL-first serving layer built on the lake foundation, well suited to dimensional models and BI workloads.偏 T-SQL、维度建模和 BI 服务层。

Closest concept: Redshift

Semantic Model

The business-facing model containing relationships, measures, security and standardized KPI definitions for Power BI.把事实表、维表、DAX 指标和权限包装成业务可理解的数据模型。

Stronger than a BI dataset

Platform and architecture questions

I would start from business domains and consumption needs, then design an integrated Fabric architecture: Data Factory for ingestion, OneLake and Lakehouse for raw and conformed Delta data, Warehouse for governed dimensional marts, and Power BI semantic models for trusted regional KPIs. I would define ownership, data contracts, security boundaries, quality SLAs and lineage from the beginning rather than treating governance as a later add-on.先按业务域和消费需求设计，再落到 Data Factory、OneLake/Lakehouse、Warehouse 和 Power BI，并从一开始定义责任、数据契约、权限、质量 SLA 与血缘。

EmphasizeRegional standards with controlled local flexibility.Your evidenceCross-border payments across countries and currencies; domain-driven warehouse experience.

I would use a Lakehouse for Spark-based engineering, Delta tables, data science and mixed file or table workloads. I would use a Warehouse for T-SQL-centric dimensional modelling, governed data marts and predictable BI serving. For this role, a common pattern is to refine data in a Lakehouse and publish trusted facts and dimensions through a Warehouse and semantic model.Lakehouse 用于 Spark、Delta 和灵活数据工程；Warehouse 用于 T-SQL、维度模型和 BI 服务。常见做法是湖仓加工、数仓发布。

The product interfaces are different, but the engineering principles are the same: ingestion patterns, incremental processing, medallion layers, dimensional modelling, Spark and SQL transformations, data quality, governance and cost control. My S3, Iceberg, Spark, Hive and warehouse experience maps directly to OneLake, Delta Lake, Fabric Spark and Warehouse. My focused learning gap is Fabric operations and advanced Power BI, not the underlying architecture.强调底层原则高度可迁移，差距主要是产品操作和 Power BI，而不是数据工程能力。

Semantic layer, BI and governance questions

A semantic model translates physical tables into a governed business model with relationships, measures, security and agreed definitions. For Chanel, metrics such as net sales, active customers, conversion and inventory performance must be consistent across markets and dashboards. The semantic model prevents each report from rebuilding business logic independently.语义模型统一关系、指标和权限，避免不同国家和报表重复定义净销售、活跃客户、转化率等指标。

I would define controls at multiple layers: schema and contract checks at ingestion, completeness and duplicate checks in the Lakehouse, source-to-target reconciliation for curated marts, semantic KPI validation before report release, and operational monitoring for freshness and SLA. Every critical issue should have an owner, severity, remediation workflow and measurable recurrence trend.质量控制要覆盖接入、湖仓、数据集市、语义指标和运行监控，并明确 owner、严重级别和整改流程。

Your evidenceT1–T5 regulatory reconciliation, AML governance and SLA improvement from 95% to above 99%.

I would combine Entra identities and least-privilege workspace or item permissions with sensitivity classification, Power BI row-level security and auditable lineage in Fabric and Purview. Access should follow business roles, and sensitive attributes should be minimized, masked or separated where appropriate. Lineage must connect the source, pipeline, curated table, semantic model and report so impact analysis is practical.用 Entra、最小权限、敏感度标签、RLS 和 Purview/Fabric 血缘管理敏感数据，并确保能做端到端影响分析。

Leadership and delivery questions

I would score initiatives by business impact, regulatory or operational risk, number of markets and users affected, dependency complexity, data readiness and delivery cost. I would balance foundational platform work with visible business outcomes, publishing a roadmap with milestones, owners, assumptions and measurable success criteria.按业务价值、风险、覆盖市场、依赖、数据成熟度和成本排序，并平衡平台基础建设与可见业务成果。

I would first document the business purpose, grain, filters, time logic and edge cases behind each definition. Then I would bring the owners together, quantify the downstream impact, agree on a canonical definition or clearly named variants, and publish the decision through the semantic layer and data catalog. The goal is not to force technical uniformity without understanding the business context.先拆解口径差异和业务目的，再推动 owner 决策，形成标准口径或清晰命名的变体，并发布到语义层和目录。

I would use my Spark SLA case: a critical job suffered severe skew, long garbage-collection pauses and occasional out-of-memory failures. I diagnosed hot keys and a large distinct aggregation, then applied configuration tuning, two-stage aggregation, targeted salting and monitoring improvements. Runtime fell from 75 to 13 minutes and on-time SLA improved from about 95% to above 99%.使用你的 Spark 倾斜与 OOM 案例，突出诊断、分阶段修复和量化结果。

In the first 30 days, I would map stakeholders, data products, platform architecture, incidents, costs and critical KPIs. By day 60, I would establish a prioritized risk and improvement backlog, clarify ownership, and deliver one visible reliability or quality improvement. By day 90, I would propose the regional roadmap, operating model, governance cadence and measurable platform KPIs.30 天理解现状，60 天形成优先级并交付一个改进，90 天给出区域路线图、运营机制和平台指标。
